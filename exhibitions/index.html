Explore the museum

Five spaces full of discovery

Each exhibition space is designed to make science visual, practical, and exciting for children, families, students, and curious visitors.

Cosmology

The Sky Above Us

Explore constellations, galaxies, early navigation, Galileo’s telescope, and the journey of solar exploration.

Evolution

Where do we come from?

Discover the origins of life, Darwin’s theory of evolution, dinosaurs, dodos, and our resident Woolly Mammoth.

Biology and Medicine

The tiny world inside us

Learn how biology and medicine changed human life, from microorganisms to penicillin and the first heart transplant.

Robotics and AI

Meet the future

Explore artificial intelligence, industrial robotics, useful home applications, and meet Rob the Robot.

Ecology

A brighter future for the planet

Understand global warming, pollution, extinction, and the positive ways we can all help create a more sustainable future.

Hands-on learning

Science is better when you can touch it

Many of our exhibits are designed for visitors to interact with, test, play with, and explore together.
